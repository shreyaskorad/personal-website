How deliberate practice reshapes workflow performance at work matters only when it changes a real decision at work, not when it adds another layer of learning activity.

A useful scenario is a weekly retrospective where the team reviews one hard call and why it succeeded or failed.

Use a short before-after comparison in team conversations so progress is linked to behavior, not presentation quality.

It also helps onboarding because new team members can see how decisions are made, reviewed, and improved.
