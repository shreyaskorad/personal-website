Field experiment pattern to surface blind spots faster for weekly business

February 21, 2026 · 2 min read · data, analytics, measurement

In data and analytics, teams get better results when they run a field experiment pattern that is explicitly designed to surface blind spots faster.

Most teams fail to improve because they publish learning assets but never connect those assets to a recurring decision checkpoint.

The better move is to attach data and analytics to one live operating rhythm where leaders can see whether behavior changed or only awareness increased.
