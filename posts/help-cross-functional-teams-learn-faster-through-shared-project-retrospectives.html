Help cross functional teams learn faster through shared project retrospectives

February 12, 2026 · 2 min read · project retrospectives, cross-functional teams, learning transfer, team learning, LxD

Cross functional teams often learn in silos during projects. Shared retrospectives can accelerate learning transfer across the organization.

Cross functional teams often learn in silos during projects. Shared retrospectives that bring together representatives from different disciplines help surface patterns and accelerate learning transfer across the organization. One product company implemented cross-team retrospectives where engineering, design, and product shared their project learnings weekly. Within months their teams reported faster innovation cycles and fewer repeated mistakes.
