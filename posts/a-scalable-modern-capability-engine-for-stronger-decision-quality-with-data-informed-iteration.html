February 27, 2026 · 2 min read · learning-design, decision-quality, modern-ld

Execution consistency drops when learning is disconnected from team decisions. In modern l&d, the strongest gains come from linking interventions to decisions reviewed weekly by the team.

In modern l&d, teams often over-index on completion while under-instrumenting the decisions that actually drive outcomes.

Start from one concrete checkpoint: the capability review meeting, and scope the first iteration to stronger decision quality.

Run this loop for two weeks: Baseline -> Experiment -> Measurement -> Iteration.
