What stronger build discipline looks like in day-to-day work

I treat what stronger build discipline looks as an operating choice: if decision quality does not improve in live work, the design is not helping.

A useful scenario is a weekly retrospective where the team reviews one hard call and why it succeeded or failed.

Keep the scope tight: one intervention, one owner, one checkpoint, and one adjustment after the first week.

It also helps onboarding because new team members can see how decisions are made, reviewed, and improved.
