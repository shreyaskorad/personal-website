Decision audit for sales enablement teams: what to inspect this week

March 5, 2026 · 2 min read · learning-design, decision-quality, decision-audit

If outcomes are flat, run a decision audit before launching another learning initiative. The bottleneck is often inside the workflow.

Which decision in the sprint planning handoff repeatedly creates rework or delay?

Write that decision as 1 sentence and tie it to 1 metric (manager coaching follow-through) with a 15-20% target.

Test one move inspired by optimizing Sales Excellence Steel Product Markets through in a single team workflow and review the behavior shift next week.

Capture three examples from the last two cycles: what was decided, who decided, and what happened next.

Then review one before/after example with the team lead before expansion.

Test one move inspired by the Accuracy-Interpretability Dilemma A Strategic Framework Navigating in a single team workflow and review the behavior shift next week.

Apply execution consistency under pressure for sales enablement teams as a single correction in the next cycle.

If the metric does not move, revise the decision checkpoint instead of adding more content.

Test this on 1 live workflow and keep only what changes observed behavior. If you run this with your team this week, track one visible behavior shift before the next review.
