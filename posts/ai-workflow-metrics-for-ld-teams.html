Workflow metrics for L&D teams

February 19, 2026 · 2 min read · workflow

AI workflow metrics for L&D teams is most credible when it improves how teams decide, align, and follow through in everyday execution.

I think ai workflow metrics for l becomes credible only when teams can point to a better judgment call in a real project moment.

I see the same pattern in weekly check-ins: people discuss progress, but they skip the decision logic that created rework.

A better move is to pick one recurring decision, define what better behavior looks like, and review that signal every week.

It also improves consistency because new team members can see how decisions are made, reviewed, and improved over time.

What decision ritual could your team improve this week to make learning visible in outcomes?
