February 27, 2026 · 2 min read · learning-design, decision-quality, gamification

Execution consistency drops when learning is disconnected from team decisions. In gamified learning, the strongest gains come from linking interventions to decisions reviewed weekly by the team.

In gamified learning, teams often over-index on completion while under-instrumenting the decisions that actually drive outcomes.
