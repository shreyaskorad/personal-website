Build feedback rituals that convert learning into visible team behavior

February 13, 2026 · 2 min read · Writing

Teams often treat feedback as a one-time event that fades quickly. Effective rituals turn learning into ongoing behavior changes.

One company implemented monthly feedback loops where teams shared what they applied from prior learning sessions. They tracked specific behaviors and checked progress at the next session. The team saw measurable behavior shifts and sustained learning adoption. Leaders also used these rituals to identify capability gaps and prioritize future development investments.

Build feedback rituals that convert learning into visible team behavior by creating recurring check-ins that track application and progress.
