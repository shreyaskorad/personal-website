Behaviour science: a habit-loop based enablement design for clear business outcomes

February 24, 2026 · 2 min read · learning-design, decision-quality, behavior-science

Capability work stalls when teams cannot link interventions to operational checkpoints. In behaviour science, results improve when teams link interventions to decisions they review weekly.

In behaviour science, teams often over-index on completion while under-instrumenting the decisions that drive outcomes. [1]
