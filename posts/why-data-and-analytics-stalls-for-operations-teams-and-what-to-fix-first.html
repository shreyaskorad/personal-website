Why data and analytics stalls for operations teams and what to fix first

March 4, 2026 · 2 min read · learning-design, decision-quality, diagnostic

When data and analytics stalls, the root issue is usually hidden in how decisions are made and reviewed, not in training volume.

Common symptom: teams report completion while manager coaching follow-through stays flat.

This usually means learning inputs are disconnected from the weekly manager one-on-one.
