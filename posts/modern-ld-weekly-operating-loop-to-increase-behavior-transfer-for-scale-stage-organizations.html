Modern L&D: weekly operating loop to increase behavior transfer for scale-stage organizations

February 21, 2026 · 2 min read · ld, learning-design, workforce

In modern l&d, teams get better results when they run a weekly operating loop that is explicitly designed to increase behavior transfer.

Most teams fail to improve because they publish learning assets but never connect those assets to a recurring decision checkpoint. [1]

The better move is to attach modern l&d to one live operating rhythm where leaders can see whether behavior changed or only awareness increased. [2]
