I treat a field note on choices as an operating choice: if decision quality does not improve in live work, the design is not helping.

Most teams lose momentum in handoffs and review meetings where people discuss outcomes but skip the decision logic behind them.

Start with one recurring decision, define what better behavior looks like, and review one visible signal in the next cycle.

That rhythm improves consistency because teams keep what works and retire what only sounds impressive.
