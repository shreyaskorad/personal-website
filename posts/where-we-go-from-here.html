Where We Go From Here

2026-02-08 · 2 min read · productivity, workflow, ai

Every single piece of work I do now starts with an IDE or an AI chat interface, and I haven't touched PowerPoint, Word, or Adobe XD in over five months.

I haven't used PowerPoint or Word in recent months. Every project now begins in an IDE or an AI chat interface. My output quality is higher. My speed is faster.

The tools I reach for have changed. An IDE opens with a file and a command. An AI chat interface waits for a prompt. Both give me what I need when I need it. No fluff. Just output.

I used to spend hours formatting slides and tweaking fonts. Now I write directly in the language that generates the final product. The bridge between thought and output has disappeared. Now I've removed it.

I started with an IDE and an AI chat interface, and I haven't gone back to PowerPoint or Word. The output is better, the speed is faster, and I'm not going back.
