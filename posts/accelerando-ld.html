Accelerando and the future of L&D

February 6, 2026 · 3 min read · AI, L&D, Strategy, Performance, Workflow

When AI is embedded in workflows, performance support is dead and L&D shifts to shaping how work gets done.

Accelerando is the sense of speed that keeps increasing. AI compresses the gap between a question and a usable answer, so the work itself becomes the classroom.

Courses still matter, but they become primers and references. The real learning happens in the flow of work, when decisions have consequences and feedback arrives immediately. AI answers in context, so support and learning collapse into the same moment.

Performance support as a separate layer is dead. AI lives inside the tools people already use. L&D designs prompts, guardrails, and examples that keep quality high while speed rises.

Measurement shifts from completion rates to time to competence, error reduction, and better judgment under pressure. Signals should show whether people can act without waiting for help.

When AI is embedded in workflows, performance support is dead, and L&D must shape the workflow itself.
