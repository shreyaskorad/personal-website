Accelerando and the future of L&D

Where AI is deeply integrated into workflows L&D shifts from delivering courses to enabling performance support.

Courses narrow in purpose to what AI cannot provide.

Courses become primers

Foundations mental models and safety nets still matter. People need frameworks before they can use AI effectively. Think of courses as reference materials rather than the full journey.

Performance support becomes AI-native

Traditional job aids lose relevance when AI is in the flow. The question shifts from supporting learners after training to how the workflow already embeds support.

Learning transfer happens in context

When learning and work are separated transfer is hard. Context native learning becomes default with micro-learnings embedded in tasks and AI bridging gaps instantly.

Design for AI-augmented performance

Consider AI's role before designing content. Focus on what AI cannot do well judgment nuance ethics and complex trade-offs.

Guardrails over content

AI handles surface level support so your role becomes about guardrails standards brand voice compliance and ethics. Less about what you teach more about ensuring AI is helpful safe and aligned.

Bottom line

Courses survive but differently. The accelerando pushes L&D toward integration first design where learning happens in the flow of work with AI as the scaffold not the replacement.
