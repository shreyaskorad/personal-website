The Challenge

Most training fails. Not because of bad content, but because of bad design.

Completion without retention

People finish courses but can't apply what they learned. Information transfer isn't learning.

Engagement without outcomes

Flashy interactions that entertain but don't teach. Gamification becomes decoration.

One-size-fits-all solutions

Generic approaches that ignore how adults actually learn and what motivates behavior change.

My Approach

Creative design meets learning science

My background in media and advertising taught me how to capture attention. Years in gamification showed me how to sustain it. And learning science ensures it all leads somewhere meaningful.

I don't just make learning look good. I design experiences where engagement serves outcomes, where every creative choice has a strategic purpose.
