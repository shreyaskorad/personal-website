## Prioritization Rules

**Safety Priority First**:
Electrical safety hazards take priority over aesthetic concerns. Diwali lights with temporary fixtures must be addressed before general cleanup to prevent potential safety incidents.

**Time-Sensitive Tasks**:
Christmas tree storage may need to be completed before weather conditions deteriorate (especially if using artificial tree) or before storage space is needed for other items.

**Resource Requirements**:
Tasks requiring electrical work should be scheduled when professional help or sufficient time is available for safe installation.

**Cleanup Sequence**:
1. Safety improvements (Diwali lights) - immediate priority
2. Christmas tree cleanup and storage - next priority
3. General holiday cleanup - final priority

**Parallel Processing**:
- Assessment and planning for Diwali lights can happen while Christmas tree storage is in progress
- Christmas tree can be cleaned while electrical safety assessment is conducted
- Multiple tasks can be performed by multiple people simultaneously
