## Decisions and Assumptions

**Decision 1: Multi-Theme Approach**
We identified 5 distinct high-potential themes rather than focusing on a single area. This approach provides flexibility for exploration based on audience interest, resource availability, and strategic priorities. Each theme offers different content opportunities and audience engagement levels.

**Decision 2: Mix of Technical and Human-Centered Themes**
The themes include both AI-focused topics (themes 1, 2, and 3) and human-centered topics (themes 4 and 5). This mix ensures content appeals to both technical and non-technical audiences while maintaining relevance across different roles and interests.

**Decision 3: Emphasis on Frameworks and Practical Application**
All identified themes will be explored through practical frameworks, actionable insights, and concrete examples. The focus is on providing real value to practitioners rather than theoretical discussions.

**Decision 4: Sustainable Long-Term Exploration**
Themes were selected based on long-term relevance rather than short-term trends. Each theme will continue to be valuable and applicable over time, allowing for ongoing exploration and development.

**Decision 5: Audience-Centric Content Development**
Content development will prioritize audience needs, interests, and pain points. Each theme will be explored in ways that provide clear value and actionable insights to the target audience.
