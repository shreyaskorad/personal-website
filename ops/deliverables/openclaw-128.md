# Trivandrum 5-Day Local Transportation Plan

## Objective

Develop a comprehensive 5-day cab transportation plan for the Trivandrum colleague wedding trip. The plan must include:
- Multiple daily transportation needs (hotel ↔ venue, hotel ↔ beaches, hotel ↔ restaurants)

## Deliverable Body

### Transportation Requirements Overview

**Trip Duration**: 3-5 days (May 1, 2026 wedding + 2-4 additional days)  
**Travelers**: 3 people (adults + 1-year-old daughter)  
**Primary Transportation**: Local cab/taxi for multi-day convenience  
**Areas to Cover**: Hotel location ↔ Wedding Venue (Al Saj Arena, Kulathur) ↔ Beaches ↔ Restaurants ↔ Shopping  

### Proposed Transportation Structure

#### Daily Trip Segments

**Day 1 - Arrival & Wedding Day:**
- Morning: Airport transfer (Trivandrum International Airport) → Hotel
- Afternoon: Hotel rest / city walk (if energy permits)
- Evening: Hotel ↔ Wedding Venue (round trip)
- Night: Hotel dining or nearby restaurant

**Day 2 - Beach Exploration:**
- Morning: Hotel ↔ Beach location (Vizhinjam/Kovalam area)
- Mid-day: Beachside restaurant lunch
- Afternoon: Beach activities + short return to hotel for rest
- Evening: Hotel ↔ Beach restaurant dinner

**Day 3 - City & Local Exploration:**
- Morning: Hotel ↔ City center sightseeing (baby-friendly spots)
- Lunch: Local restaurant near sightseeing area
- Afternoon: Return to hotel for rest
- Evening: Premium dinner outing

**Day 4-5 - Leisure & Departure:**
- Option A: Additional beach visits or nature spots
- Option B: Shopping trips (baby clothes, souvenirs)
- Morning/Afternoon: Local travel between locations
- Evening: Hotel checkout + Airport transfer (if departing Day 5)

### Cab Package Options

#### Option 1: Daily Rental with Driver
- **Structure**: Rent cab with dedicated driver for each day
- **Advantages**: Flexibility to stop as needed, driver familiar with baby care
- **Estimated Daily Cost**: ₹1,500-2,500 (based on typical Indian city rates)
- **Total for 5 days**: ₹7,500-12,500
- **Includes**: Full-day use, driver tips, basic fuel

#### Option 2: Package Deal (Recommended)
- **Structure**: Pre-booked 5-day package at negotiated rate
- **Advantages**: Fixed cost, driver reliability, potential discounts
- **Estimated Package Cost**: ₹6,000-10,000 for 5 days
- **Inclusions**: Unlimited rides within city, driver per day, fuel, tolls

#### Option 3: Mixed Transportation
- **Structure**: Combine cab with walking for shorter distances
- **Advantages**: Cost savings for shorter trips, more flexibility
- **Estimated Savings**: ₹2,000-4,000 over full cab package
