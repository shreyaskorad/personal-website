### Review Process Overview

The operations dashboard review (Task Review #43) was initiated to assess current dashboard functionality, identify improvement opportunities, and ensure alignment with operational needs. The review examined dashboard metrics, visualization clarity, data accuracy, and user experience considerations across all operational views and reports.

### Key Findings and Improvements Identified

**Data Presentation Issues**
- Metric definitions lacked contextual clarity across multiple dashboard views
- Time period filters inconsistent with operational reporting needs
- Data aggregation methods required refinement for relevant business segments

**User Experience Gaps**
- Navigation between operational views created unnecessary friction
- Customization options insufficient for different user roles
- Loading times and performance bottlenecks affecting daily usage

**Visual Hierarchy Problems**
- Critical metrics not immediately visible in primary views
- Color coding and visual signals inconsistent with operational alerting needs
- Information density compromised readability and decision-making speed
